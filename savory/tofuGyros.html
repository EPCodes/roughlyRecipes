Tofu Gyros

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Coat tofu slices thoroughly and marinate for at least 30 minutes or overnight.
  3. Cook tofu: pan-fry over medium heat until golden, or bake at 400°F for 20–25 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  4. Serve in warm pita with lettuce, tomato, red onion, and tzatziki sauce.
