Red Lentil Dal

Recipe by Hari Ghotra

Ingredients:

Instructions:

  1. Place lentils, salt, and water in a pan. Bring to a boil, skim off froth, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes until soft.
  2. In a frying pan, heat oil or ghee. Add dried chilli, bay leaf, and cumin seeds; cook until seeds sizzle.
  3. Remove chilli and mince
  4. Add onion and garlic, frying until lightly browned. Reduce heat and stir in tomatoes, ginger, turmeric, fenugreek, and chopped chilli. Cook down for 10 minutes to form a thick masala paste.
  5. Add a ladle of cooked lentils to the masala paste and mix. Return all contents to the main lentil pan and stir well.
  6. Adjust consistency like a thick soupâ€”add boiling water if too thick or simmer to reduce.
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning. Stir in garam masala and coriander before serving.
